This is a webpage where I going to show some animals and their abilities...

  • The iguana is a large, green lizard found in tropical areas.
  • It has a long tail, strong legs, and spiky crests on its back.
  • Iguanas love to eat leaves, fruits, and flowers.
  • They are calm creatures and enjoy sunbathing on rocks or trees.
  • The giraffe is the tallest land animal in the world.
  • It has a long neck that helps it reach leaves high in trees.
  • Giraffes have spotted coats and gentle, calm behavior.
  • They use their strong legs to walk long distances across the savannah.
  • The macaw is a colorful parrot known for its bright feathers.
  • It lives in rainforests and loves to fly high through the trees.
  • Macaws are smart birds and can mimic human speech.
  • They enjoy eating fruits, nuts, and seeds with their strong beaks.
  • The tiger is a powerful wild cat with orange fur and black stripes.
  • It lives in forests and hunts alone, mainly at night.
  • Tigers are excellent swimmers and love water.
  • They are strong, fast, and known as the kings of the jungle.
